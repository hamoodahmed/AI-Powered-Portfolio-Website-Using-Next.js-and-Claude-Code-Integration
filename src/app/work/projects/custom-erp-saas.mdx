## Overview
Working with international clients at Codes Soft, I developed a specialized ERP module to handle complex SaaS billing cycles that the standard ERPNext didn't support out of the box.

## Key Features
- **Automated Recurring Billing**: Integration with Stripe and PayPal for seamless subscription management.
- **Custom Financial Reports**: Real-time MRR (Monthly Recurring Revenue) and Churn tracking.
- **Dynamic DocTypes**: Tailored business logic for SaaS-specific workflows.

## Technical Stack
- **Framework**: Frappe / ERPNext
- **Language**: Python, JavaScript
- **Database**: MariaDB
