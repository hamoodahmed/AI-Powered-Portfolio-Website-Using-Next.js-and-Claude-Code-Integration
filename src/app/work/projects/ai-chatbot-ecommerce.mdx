## Overview
Built for an e-commerce store with 10,000+ monthly visitors. The chatbot handles 70% of customer queries without human intervention, providing instant responses 24/7.

## Key Features
- **Natural Language Understanding**: Uses Claude to understand customer intent even with typos and slang.
- **Real-Time Data**: Connects to inventory and order management systems for live updates.
- **Escalation Logic**: Automatically transfers complex queries to human agents.
- **Multi-Language Support**: Handles English and Urdu conversations.

## Technical Stack
- **AI**: Claude 3.5 Sonnet
- **Framework**: LangChain for conversation management
- **Backend**: Python (FastAPI)
- **Frontend**: React widget embedded in Shopify store
- **Database**: MongoDB for conversation history

## Business Impact
- 70% of queries resolved without human intervention
- Average response time: 2 seconds
- Customer satisfaction score: 4.6/5
- Support team workload reduced by 60%
